# CSC-403---Project
Software Engineering Project - Ad Tinder

This Project is focused on creating an Ad Tinder game that allows you to log in and swipe through ads to finder your perfect product.
You swipe right to say "YES" and swipe left say "NO" to a large selection of ads.

You can donate and if you click on the screen it will bring you to our partner page.

Finally, after going through all of our ads you will be given your own "Perfect Ad" based on your choices.

Thank you for playing!
